"Imagine flying through the sky like that. Can you see it little buddy?... We just have to keep dreaming. We just have to keep believing. Soon I'll take you from all this pain and I'll build you a kingdom of your own. You will be a king. I promise. Even if it kills me to do it, I will make you a king. Just stay with me for a little longer. Don't close those eyes. Don't give up just yet. There is a sun over the Horizon. we just need to keep pushing for it".

They braved many trials in their attempts to reach their goal. Bitter cold and scorching heat. The elder of the two fought off many grave dangers to keep the little one safe. Taking one wound after another the elder kept pushing on. He fought. He bled. He screamed. He cried. He worked so hard and suddenly he realized he had reached his limit. His sword fell from his trembling fingers and he realized he couldn't go anymore. He was done. Lost in the woods with no food and nothing to show for his efforts the elder past unconcious hoping to never wake again. The younger realizing all that had been done took up his brother's sword and placed the elder brother atop a makeshift sled. He drug the elder and fought off all that tried to harm them. The younger realized the older was in dier need of help, made camp and did all he could to bring the elder back from his slumber, but the elder would not wake.... The sun then shone down over the horizon. It's light crept across the elders body slowly incasing him in stone. The younger screamed, "NO!!!" as he saw a smile creep over the elder's face, and a look of relief. "Keep pushing brother... Your nearly there. I'm so proud of you... I'll.. be... watching..."
